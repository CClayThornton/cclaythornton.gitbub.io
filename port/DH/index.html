Home

Digital Humanities is a broad, multidisciplinary field that combines the use of digital tools and the study of the humanities to analyze, store, interpret, and create a wide breadth of writings, images, and other cultural objects.


This homepage outlines the rest of the website. First, under the "Definition" page, you will find a succinct description of digital humanities.


Under the “Tools and Techniques” page, you can find information over the tools used by researchers in this field.


“Examples” lists a series of projects in the digital humanities.


Finally, “Resources” shows the sources used for this site which also acts as a list of further readings for those interested.
